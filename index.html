The Concentric Music Project: a website documenting the music of an experimental jazz/rock project from Austin TX (2002-2007)

from the last Promo Package, 2006

Concentric is an Austin band that breaks ground with a unique blend of acid jazz, free-funk, and math-rock. The project has in fact been around Austin for 5 years—going through phases of hibernation and frenetic gigging. The band is surfacing again to mash up common musical genres into a fresh and cohesive musical style in itself. The four current members have spiraled through the country – Philly, Boulder, Cincinnati, Boston, Mississippi, West Texas - all along the way absorbing influences & ideas to utilize in Austin, resulting in an ideal instrumental brew of intellect and intensity. The group captures elements from influences such as electric Medeski Martin and Wood, DJ Spooky, Critters Buggin’, and Tortoise. Weilding live spontaneity and modern technology, the band produces fresh and defiant melodies over infectious grooves.
